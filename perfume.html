2.0 Perfume

Oil Perfumery Dupe of Tom Ford Lost Cherry

This is my baby. My #1 since high school, my forever favorite. I have two vials of it. So cheap for something sooo long-lasting and amazingly scented. I am not normally a gourmand-man, but something about the liquer notes in this helps mature the cherry notes. Dries down as a stunning almond on my skin. I say this is my fall/winter scent but really I wear it all year. Love.

HM by Hanae Mori

Fragrance for men created by Japanese perfumery Hanae Mori. I start wearing this once it gets warm. Smells like lavendar, lemons, and wood. Masculine but not old-spice-axe-body-spray-manly. Big fan!

Fils De Dieu Du Riz Et Des Agrumes by Etat Libre D'Orange

A recent favorite! Love Etat Libre D'Orange's sample collections. This is a limey coconut that works great in spring/summer. Smells like soap in a good way. I am always a fan of a cardamom note...
