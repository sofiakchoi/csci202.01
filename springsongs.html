3.0 Spring Songs

我只在乎你 by Teresa Teng

A classic! Teresa Teng, Queen of Asian Pop... This song is best listened to in the morning while you imagine it's 1996 and you are in Hong Kong about to fall in love with a boy you work at a McDonalds with.

Young Adult Friction by The Pains of Being Pure At Heart

Listen in the afternoon while sitting outside in the sun. Watch people walk by. This song makes me feel like its 2012.

Elevator Love Letter by Stars

Ok this is a night-time spring song. It's a little warm and you're walking home around dusk. Everything smells green.
